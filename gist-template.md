LOOK AHEAD AND LOOK BEHIND

## Summary

In this regex we will be going over how to properly look ahead and behind any 'T' in a sentence.

### Look-ahead and Look-behind

LOOK AHEAD = ``` (?<=[tT]he). ```

LOOK BEHIND = ``` (?=[tT]he). ```

The cat sat down by my feet.
